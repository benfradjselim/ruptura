## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors with sustained contributions may be nominated.
Requires approval from existing maintainers.

## Decision Making

Lazy consensus. If no objections within 72 hours, proposal passes.
Failing consensus, majority vote of maintainers decides.

## Release Process

Semantic versioning. Each release tagged and signed.
Changelog maintained in CHANGELOG.md.
